Discover the Universe of Sci-Fi Books

Welcome to Galactic Readers book club, where we gather monthly to read and chat about sci-fi books, host author events and engage in lively discussion. while our focus is sci-fi, all are welcome to join us through the pages of captivating books

Our Readers Recommended

2001: A Space Odyssey

By Arthur C. Clarke

On the Moon, an enigma is uncovered. So great are the implications of this discovery that for the first time men are sent out deep into our solar system. But long before their destination is reached, things begin to go horribly, inexplicably wrong...

Dune

By Frank Herbert

Set on the desert planet Arrakis, Dune is the story of the boy Paul Atreides, heir to a noble family tasked with ruling an inhospitable world where the only thing of value is the “spice” melange, a drug capable of extending life and enhancing consciousness. Coveted across the known universe, melange is a prize worth killing for...

The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

By Douglas Adams

Seconds before the Earth is demolished to make way for a galactic freeway, Arthur Dent is plucked off the planet by his friend Ford Prefect, a researcher for the revised edition of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy who, for the last fifteen years, has been posing as an out-of-work actor.

Project Hail Mary

By Andy Weir

The story of Ryland Grace, a sole survivor on a desperate mission to save humanity and Earth. He wakes up millions of miles from home, with amnesia and only two corpses for company, tasked with thwarting an extinction-level threat to the species.
